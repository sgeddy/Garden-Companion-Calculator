Compatibility Calculator


HOW TO USE:

1. Click 'Add Plant' until you have a row for each plant you would like to grow in your garden or raised bed. To delete a row, select the checkbox for that row and press 'Delete Plant'.
2. Select the plants you would like to grow from the dropdown in each row under the Plants column.
3. Click the CHECK COMPATIBILITY button when you are finished and review your results.
